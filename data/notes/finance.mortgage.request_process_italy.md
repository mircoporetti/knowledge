
## Steps

1. **Shop around for the best offer** - Visit multiple banks to compare offers vs. relying on a broker

2. **Include a clausola sospensiva in the purchase proposal** - When making the purchase proposal at the real estate agency, include a *clausola sospensiva* (suspensive clause) that clearly specifies:
   - The mortgage amount
   - What the clause is tied to (income approval - *delibera reddituale* - or actual mortgage obtainment)

3. **Request official confirmation** - Ask for official confirmation that the mortgage application has been submitted

4. **Istruttoria (preliminary assessment)** - The bank evaluates whether you qualify for the mortgage and what monthly payment you can afford. Factors considered:
   - Salary and employment type (employee vs. *partita IVA* / self-employed)
   - Cost of living in the area
   - The mortgage payment cannot exceed the subsistence threshold based on the area
   - Number of dependents
   - Credit history (any unpaid installments or other issues?)
   - Other ongoing loans or financing

5. **Perizia (property appraisal)** - The bank's technical expert appraises the property. If the property value is estimated lower than the purchase price, the bank may grant a smaller mortgage

6. **Atto di mutuo (mortgage deed)** - Final signing of the mortgage deed before the notary
